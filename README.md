# 🛒 Amazon Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a **comprehensive Power BI dashboard** for analyzing Amazon product sales data.
The dashboard provides clear insights into **sales performance, pricing strategies, discounts, and customer behavior**.

The goal of this project is to transform raw data into **actionable business insights** through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📊 Dataset

* Source: Kaggle Amazon Sales Dataset
* Includes:

  * Product Name
  * Main Category & Sub Category
  * Actual Price & Discounted Price
  * Discount Percentage
  * Ratings & Reviews

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze product sales performance
* Understand the impact of discounts on sales
* Evaluate customer ratings and reviews
* Compare categories and subcategories
* Identify top-performing products

---

## 🧠 Key KPIs

* 📦 Total Products
* 💰 Total Sales
* 📊 Total Product Sales
* ⭐ Average Rating
* 🏷️ Average Discount %
* 💵 Average Actual Price
* 💸 Average Discounted Price

## 💡 Insights & Findings

* Electronics category dominates total sales
* Discounts influence sales but not always directly
* Some products have high ratings but low sales
* Pricing plays a key role in customer decisions
